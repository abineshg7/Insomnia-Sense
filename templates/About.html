About Insomnia and Our System

What is Insomnia?

Insomnia is a sleep disorder that affects millions of people worldwide, making it difficult to fall asleep, stay asleep, or wake up too early. Chronic insomnia can lead to serious health issues, including mental stress, anxiety, depression, and decreased productivity.

How Our System Helps

Using cutting-edge machine learning techniques, our Insomnia Prediction System leverages Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) models to predict insomnia risk based on various user inputs.
